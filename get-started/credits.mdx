## Free Plan:

Get started with Prem for free! Experience core features without any commitment.

**$0/500 Credits monthly**

**Equivalent to:**

- **500 Requests** from **Low Tier** Models 
- **50 Requests** from **Medium Tier** Models
<Warning>
High Tier Models are not available in **Free Plan**
</Warning> 
## Pro Plan: 

**$20/2,000 Credits monthly**

**Equivalent to:**

- **2,000 Requests** from **Low Tier** Models
- **200 Requests** from **Medium Tier** Models  
- **20 Requests** from **High Tier** Models
